Welcome to the TripWeather application!

    
Available Routes:

    
    api/v1.0/precipitation  
    All available Dates with the total precipitation on each day

    /api/v1.0/stations 
    List of stations collecting the weather
    
     /api/v1.0/tobs    
    Returns the last year's dates and temperatures  

    /api/v1.0/start
    Enter a start date where it says 'start'to see temp statistics since then 
    Date range ends on August 23 2017 
    Enter dates in YYYY-MM-DD format

    /api/v1.0/start/end 
    Enter a start and end dates where it says 'start' and 'end' to get temp statistics for that range 
    Enter dates in YYYY-MM-DD format
